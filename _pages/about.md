Welcome. I am a Biostatistician working at the interface of spatial genomics, microbiome data science, and cancer biology.
I am currently a Research Fellow working with [Professor Evan T. Keller]([https://xiangzhou.github.io/](https://medschool.umich.edu/profile/1464/evan-t-keller) in the Division of Urologic Oncology, Department of Urology, University of Michigan. 

Before joining the Department of Urology, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Biostatistics at the University of Michigan, where I worked with [Professor Xiang Zhou](https://xiangzhou.github.io/). I received my Ph.D. in Biostatistics from Pennsylvania State University under the supervision of [Professor Xiang Zhan](https://sites.google.com/view/zhangroup/home). My dissertation focused on adaptive statistical methods for microbiome association analysis. I also hold a Master of Statistics degree from the Indian Statistical Institute, and a B.Sc. with Honours in Statistics from Asutosh College, University of Calcutta.
